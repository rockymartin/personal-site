About Me

Born and raised in Las Vegas, Nevada, I left the southwest to attend Boston University , where I earned a BS in Business Administration with a concentration in Finance. During college I did everything from food service to insurance sales to career skills coaching, and after graduating in December 2016, I started as an analyst at Liberty Mutual. I spent a year in their Analyst Development Program, building many of the core business skills I utilize today, and moved to Criteo after the following year there.

Currently, my time is split half-and-half between making and managing , as I serve as the primary gatekeeper between a team of associate-level analysts and a large sales & account management organization with a multitude of data-driven needs. On top of that, I spend the rest of my time on project work related to important strategic relationships and high opportunity process improvements .

When I'm not at work, you can catch me at a show at The Sinclair (usually a Night Shift Whirpool in hand), at Veggie Galaxy getting a vegan omelet or BLT, at community meetings on housing or transit issues, or enjoying a midnight movie at Coolidge Corner.
